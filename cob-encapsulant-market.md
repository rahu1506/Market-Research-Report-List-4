<p><p>The COB encapsulant market can be divided into two main types: silicone and epoxy. Silicone encapsulants are known for their flexibility, high temperature resistance, and UV stability. They are often used in applications where thermal conductivity and moisture resistance are important. On the other hand, epoxy encapsulants are preferred for their high adhesion strength, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. They are commonly used in harsh environmental conditions where protection against moisture, chemicals, and physical stress is necessary. These two types of encapsulants cater to different requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
