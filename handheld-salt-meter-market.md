<p><p>Handheld salt meters are devices used to measure the salinity of liquids such as water. There are three main types of handheld salt meters on the market: Optical, Conductivity, and Hydrometer. Optical salt meters measure salinity by analyzing the light passing through a sample. Conductivity salt meters measure salinity based on the electrical conductivity of a solution. Hydrometer salt meters measure salinity by assessing the density of a liquid. Each type of salt meter has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
