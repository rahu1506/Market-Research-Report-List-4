<p><strong>The Deep Sea Mining Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cobalt rich crusts</li><li>Manganese nodules</li><li>Seafloor massive sulphides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep sea mining technology market includes three main types: cobalt rich crusts, manganese nodules, and seafloor massive sulphides. Cobalt rich crusts are formed on seamounts and contain valuable metals like cobalt, nickel, and platinum. Manganese nodules are found on the seafloor and contain manganese, nickel, copper, and cobalt. Seafloor massive sulphides are formed at hydrothermal vents and contain metals like copper, gold, silver, and zinc. Each market type presents unique opportunities for extracting valuable resources from the ocean floor.</p></p>
