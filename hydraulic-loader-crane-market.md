<p><p>There are two main types of hydraulic loader crane markets: knuckle boom cranes and telescopic cranes. Knuckle boom cranes have a jointed arm that can fold into a compact space when not in use, making them ideal for working in tight spaces. Telescopic cranes have a straight arm that extends and retracts, allowing for increased reach and lifting capacity. Both types of cranes offer distinct advantages and cater to different industries and applications within the construction and logistics sectors.</p></p>
