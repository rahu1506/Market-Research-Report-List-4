<p><ul><li>Synthetic/Chemical API</li><li>Biological API</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small molecular API market can be categorized into two main types: Synthetic/Chemical API and Biological API. Synthetic/Chemical APIs are manufactured through chemical processes in the laboratory, while Biological APIs are produced through biological processes using living organisms such as bacteria, yeast, or mammalian cells. Synthetic/Chemical APIs are often preferred for their scalability, cost-effectiveness, and well-established manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Biological APIs offer advantages such as higher specificity, reduced side effects, and potential for biotechnology innovations. Both market types play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
