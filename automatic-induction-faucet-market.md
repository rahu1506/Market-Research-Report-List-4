<p><p>Automatic induction faucets can be categorized into two main types - Infrared Sensor Faucets and Touch Sensor Faucets. Infrared Sensor Faucets use infrared technology to detect the presence of hands or objects within close proximity and automatically turn on the water flow. Touch Sensor Faucets, on the other hand, are activated by a simple touch on the faucet spout or handle. Both types offer hands-free operation, promote hygiene, and conserve water, making them popular choices in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
