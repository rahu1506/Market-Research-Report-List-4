<p><p>Steel hinges can be categorized into two main types: sliding type and card slot type. Sliding type hinges allow for smooth and effortless opening and closing of doors or cabinets by sliding horizontally. On the other hand, card slot type hinges have a mechanism that allows them to swing open and closed like a door on a hinge. Both types of hinges offer durability and strength due to their steel construction, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
