<p><p>Wooden pallet collars come in two main types: foldable and unfoldable. Foldable pallet collars are designed to collapse flat for efficient storage and transportation when not in use, saving space and reducing shipping costs. On the other hand, unfoldable pallet collars are constructed as a solid structure that does not fold down. They provide a sturdy and secure containment for goods during storage and transportation, but do not offer the same space-saving benefits as foldable collars. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
