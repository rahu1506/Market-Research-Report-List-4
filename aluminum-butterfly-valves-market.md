<p><p>Aluminum butterfly valves are a specific type of valve used to control the flow of liquids or gases in a piping system. They consist of a rotating disc or plate which is mounted on a shaft. When the valve is closed, the disc is perpendicular to the flow, blocking it completely. When the valve is open, the disc is parallel to the flow, allowing for unrestricted flow.</p><p>The global aluminum butterfly valves market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for aluminum butterfly valves can be attributed to factors such as their durability, low maintenance requirements, and ability to handle high-pressure applications.
