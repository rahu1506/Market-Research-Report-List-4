<p><p>Commercial power generators are classified into two main types: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are mobile units that can be easily transported and used at remote locations or job sites. They are typically smaller in size and provide temporary power during emergencies or outdoor activities. Standby generators, on the other hand, are permanent fixtures that are connected to a building's electrical system. They automatically turn on during power outages to provide continuous backup power. Both types serve important purposes in ensuring a reliable power supply for commercial establishments.</p></p>
