<p><p>Die casting lubricants are essential in the manufacturing process to reduce friction, prevent wear on die components, and improve overall production efficiency. There are three main types of die casting lubricants: die lubricants, which are applied directly to the die surface; plunger lubricants, which are used to lubricate the plunger during the casting process; and other lubricants, which include release agents, anti-solder compounds, and specialty lubricants tailored to specific casting applications. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring high-quality die casted products.</p></p>
