<p><p>Single-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps are used in applications requiring low to medium vacuum levels. They consist of a single rotor and stator system, offering simple operation and maintenance. Two-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps are designed for high vacuum applications. They feature two sets of rotor and stator systems, allowing for higher compression ratios and better efficiency in reaching deeper vacuum levels. Both types of pumps are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemical processing, and semiconductor manufacturing for various vacuum applications.</p></p>
