<p><strong>The Forged Steel Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bolted Bonnet</li><li>Welded Bonnet</li><li>Pressure Seal Bonnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forged steel gate valves are available in three main types based on their bonnet design: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. Bolted bonnet gate valves have a bolted connection between the body and bonnet, making them easy to maintain and repair. Welded bonnet gate valves have a welded connection between the body and bonnet, providing a strong and leak-proof seal. Pressure seal bonnet gate valves are designed for high-pressure applications, with a bonnet that seals under pressure to prevent leaks.</p></p>
