<p><p>A Dry Ice MicroParticle Blasting Machine is a type of cleaning equipment that uses dry ice pellets as a blasting agent to clean surfaces. This physical reaction produces micro-explosions that effectively remove contaminants and residues without causing damage to the underlying surface.</p><p>The Dry Ice MicroParticle Blasting Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
