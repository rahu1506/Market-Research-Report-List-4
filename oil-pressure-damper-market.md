<p><p>Oil pressure dampers are categorized into two types: horizontal dampers and vertical dampers. Horizontal dampers are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications where space limitations exist. They are designed to handle the forces exerted horizontally on the system. Vertical dampers are utilized in applications where the force is exerted vertically. Both types of dampers work by regulating the flow of oil to absorb and dissipate energy, thereby reducing vibrations and ensuring smooth operation of the system.</p></p>
