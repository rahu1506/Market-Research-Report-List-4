<p><p>Two-piece tire molds are commonly used in the manufacturing of tires for vehicles. There are two main types of two-piece tire molds - wrought aluminium tire molds and steel tire molds. Wrought aluminium tire molds are known for their lightweight and high heat conductivity, making them ideal for producing tires with intricate designs. On the other hand, steel tire molds are preferred for their durability and ability to withstand high pressures during the tire molding process. Both types of molds play a crucial role in the tire manufacturing industry.</p></p>
