<p><p>Holographic Concave Grating market can be categorized into two types: Transmission Grating and Reflective Grating. Transmission Grating uses a series of parallel lines to disperse light while allowing it to pass through, typically used in spectroscopy. On the other hand, Reflective Grating reflects light back towards the source, commonly used in laser systems. These two types of gratings cater to different applications and industries, providing a diverse range of options for optical dispersion and manipulation.</p></p>
