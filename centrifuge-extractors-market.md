<p><p>Centrifuge extractors are commonly categorized into two main types: monostage and multistage. Monostage centrifuge extractors involve a single stage of separation, where the mixture is spun at high speeds to separate the components based on their density. On the other hand, multistage centrifuge extractors involve multiple stages of separation, allowing for more precise and efficient extraction of components. Both types of extractors are widely used in various industries for separating solid and liquid components in mixtures.</p></p>
