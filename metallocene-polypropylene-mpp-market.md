<p><p>Metallocene Polypropylene (mPP) is a type of polypropylene produced using metallocene catalyst technology, resulting in improved properties compared to conventional polypropylene. There are two main types of mPP: Metallocene Isotactic polypropylene (miPP) and Metallocene Syndiotactic Polypropylene (msPP). miPP has a higher melting point and better impact resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring strength and durability. On the other hand, msPP has improved clarity, stiffness, and heat resistance, making it ideal for packaging and automotive applications.</p></p>
