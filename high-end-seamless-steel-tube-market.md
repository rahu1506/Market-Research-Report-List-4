<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Seamless Steel Tube</li><li>Cold Rolled Seamless Steel Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-end seamless steel tube market is divided into two main types: hot rolled seamless steel tube and cold rolled seamless steel tube. Hot rolled seamless steel tubes are produced by heating a solid billet and then rolling it to form a tube shape, resulting in a rough surface finish. Cold rolled seamless steel tubes are produced by passing a solid billet through a series of rollers at room temperature, resulting in a smoother surface finish. Both types are used in various industries for their high strength and durability.</p></p>
