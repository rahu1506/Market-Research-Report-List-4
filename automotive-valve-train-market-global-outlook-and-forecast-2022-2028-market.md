<p><p>The Automotive Valve Train Market is segmented into two main types: OHV (Overhead Valve) and OHC (Overhead Cam) systems. The OHV system uses a camshaft located within the engine block, operating the valves through lifters and pushrods. On the other hand, the OHC system has the camshaft positioned above the valves in the cylinder head, directly actuating them. Both systems play a crucial role in controlling the intake and exhaust flow of gases within the engine, ultimately impacting its performance and efficiency.</p></p>
