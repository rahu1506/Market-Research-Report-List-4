<p><strong>The Kidney Hemodialysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kidney hemodialysis equipment can be categorized into two main types: single pump and double pump systems. Single pump systems use one pump to circulate blood and dialysate, while double pump systems use two pumps for separate circulation of blood and dialysate. Single pump systems are simpler and more cost-effective, while double pump systems offer more precise control over fluid removal and are often used for patients with complex treatment needs. Both types of equipment are essential for providing life-saving hemodialysis treatment to patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
