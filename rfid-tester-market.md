<p><ul><li>Low Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>High Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>Ultra-High Frequency RFID Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tester market can be categorized into three main types: Low Frequency RFID Tester, High Frequency RFID Tester, and Ultra-High Frequency RFID Tester. Low Frequency RFID Testers are designed to test RFID tags operating at frequencies below 135 kHz, High Frequency RFID Testers are for tags operating between 13.56 MHz and 900 MHz, and Ultra-High Frequency RFID Testers are used for tags operating above 900 MHz. Each type of tester is tailored to specific frequency ranges to accurately test and verify RFID technology performance.</p></p>
