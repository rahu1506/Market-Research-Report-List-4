<p><p>Direct-to-consumer genetic sequencing market includes various types such as diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening involves analyzing an individual's DNA to identify genetic mutations that could predispose to certain diseases. PGD is used in assisted reproduction to select embryos free of genetic disorders before implantation. Relationship testing determines genetic relatedness between individuals, commonly used in paternity testing. These services provide consumers with valuable insights into their genetic makeup and potential health risks.</p></p>
