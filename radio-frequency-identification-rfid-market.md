Tags are electronic devices attached to objects to store and transmit information. Readers are devices that read the information on the tags. Software is the technology that manages and analyses the data collected by the tags and readers. The RFID market is growing rapidly as businesses across various industries adopt this technology for inventory management, tracking, and security purposes.</p></p>
