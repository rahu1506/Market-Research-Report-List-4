<p><p>Mobile Platelet Collection System market consists of two main types: Centrifugation and Membrane Filtration. Centrifugation involves spinning blood samples in a centrifuge to separate platelets from other blood components. Membrane Filtration uses specialized filters to separate platelets based on size. Both technologies are used in portable systems that allow for on-site collection of platelets, offering advantages of convenience and efficiency for healthcare professionals. These systems play a crucial role in providing a readily available supply of platelets for various medical procedures.</p></p>
