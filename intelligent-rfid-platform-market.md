<p><strong>The Intelligent RFID Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF) RFID</li><li>High-Frequency (HF) RFID</li><li>Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent RFID Platform Market is classified into three types based on frequency bands - Low Frequency (LF) RFID, High-Frequency (HF) RFID, and Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID. LF RFID operates at a frequency range of 30 KHz to 300 KHz, suitable for short-range applications. HF RFID operates at 3-30 MHz, providing a balance between range and efficiency. UHF RFID operates at 300 MHz to 3 GHz, offering long-range capabilities for high-speed reading. Each type has specific applications and advantages based on frequency band characteristics.</p></p>
