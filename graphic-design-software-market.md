<p><ul><li>Pixel-based Image Editors</li><li>Vector-based Image Editors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pixel-based Image Editors are graphic design software that operates on individual pixels, allowing users to edit images at a granular level. They are ideal for tasks such as photo editing and digital painting. On the other hand, Vector-based Image Editors use mathematical equations to create shapes and lines, making them ideal for tasks such as logo design and illustration. Both market types cater to different design needs, with pixel-based editors focusing on detailed editing and vector-based editors focusing on scalable and precise design.</p></p>
