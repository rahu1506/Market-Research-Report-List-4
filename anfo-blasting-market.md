<p><p>ANFO blasting market consists of three main types: Powdered Ammonium Nitrate Oil Explosive, Porous Granular Ammonium Nitrate Oil Explosive, and Heavy Ammonium Nitrate Oil Explosive. Powdered ANFO is finely ground for better mixing and handling. Porous granular ANFO has added pores for increased sensitivity and detonation efficiency. Heavy ANFO is denser and more powerful, suitable for deeper blasts. Each type caters to specific blasting needs in industries like mining, construction, and quarrying, offering varying levels of strength and precision.</p></p>
