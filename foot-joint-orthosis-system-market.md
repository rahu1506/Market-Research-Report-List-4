<p><p>Foot Joint Orthosis System includes two main market types - Inlay Type and Externally Assisted Type. Inlay Type orthoses are customized to fit inside the shoe and provide support to the foot joints. They are discreet and comfortable for everyday use. Externally Assisted Type orthoses are worn on the outside of the shoe and provide additional support to the foot joints through straps and braces. They are often used for more severe cases of foot joint instability and require adjustments by a healthcare professional for proper fit and function.</p></p>
