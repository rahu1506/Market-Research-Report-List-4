<p><p>Dip Welded Wire Mesh is divided into two main market types: Electro Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh and Hot Dipped Galvanized Steel Mesh. Electro Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh is coated with a thin layer of zinc through an electroplating process, providing a moderate level of corrosion resistance. On the other hand, Hot Dipped Galvanized Steel Mesh is coated by immersing the mesh in molten zinc, creating a thicker and more durable protective layer against rust and corrosion. Both types of wire mesh are commonly used in construction, agriculture, and industrial applications.</p></p>
