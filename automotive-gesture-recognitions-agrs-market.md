<p><p>Automotive Gesture Recognition Systems come in two main types: Touch Based Systems and Touchless Systems. Touch Based Systems require physical contact with a surface to operate, such as tapping a screen or button. Touchless Systems, on the other hand, use sensors to detect gestures in the air, allowing for hands-free control of features like volume adjustment or answering calls. Both types of systems are gaining popularity in the automotive industry for their convenience and safety benefits.</p></p>
