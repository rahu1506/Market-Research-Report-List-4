<p><ul><li>One-dimensional</li><li>Two-dimensional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rod lens arrays are classified into two main types: one-dimensional and two-dimensional. One-dimensional rod lens arrays are arranged along a single axis, providing a linear array of lenses. They are commonly used in applications such as barcode scanners and alignment systems. On the other hand, two-dimensional rod lens arrays are arranged in a grid pattern, providing a two-dimensional array of lenses. They are often used in applications such as medical imaging and laser scanning systems where precise focusing and imaging capabilities are required.</p></p>
