<p><strong>The Belt Drives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ZV</li><li>CV</li><li>CR</li><li>CL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt drives are classified into different types based on the design of their belts: ZV, CV, CR, and CL. ZV belts have a trapezoidal cross-section and are commonly used in light-duty applications. CV belts have a circular cross-section and are suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. CR belts have a cogged design for improved grip and efficiency. CL belts have a flat design and are often used in power transmission systems. Each type of belt drive offers unique characteristics and benefits for various applications.</p></p>
