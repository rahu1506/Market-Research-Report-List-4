<p><p>Vacuum tubular solar water heaters come in three main types: all glass vacuum tube, heat pipe vacuum tube, and U-shaped vacuum tube. All glass vacuum tubes are durable and have high heat absorption efficiency. Heat pipe vacuum tubes use a copper heat pipe to transfer heat from the vacuum tube to the water tank, increasing efficiency. U-shaped vacuum tubes are designed for colder climates, as their unique shape prevents freezing and thermal shock damage. Each type serves different purposes based on climate and energy needs.</p></p>
