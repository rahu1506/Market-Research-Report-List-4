<p><p>Microwave Integrated Circuits (MIC) market encompasses Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits (MMIC) and Hybrid Microwave Integrated Circuits (HMIC). MMICs are integrated circuits with all components on a single substrate, offering high performance and reliability. HMICs combine the advantages of both MMICs and discrete circuit components, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types are widely used in applications requiring high-frequency signal processing, such as radar systems, communication networks, and satellite communications. The MIC market continues to grow due to advancements in technology and increasing demand for high-speed data transfer.</p></p>
