<p><p>Flight Data Monitoring System (FDMS) can be categorized into two main types: On Board and On Ground systems. On Board systems are installed directly on the aircraft and continuously monitor and record flight data in real-time. This data is then analyzed to improve safety, efficiency, and maintenance procedures. On Ground systems, on the other hand, receive data retrieved from the aircraft after landing. This data is then analyzed for safety and maintenance purposes, providing valuable insights to airlines and aviation authorities.</p></p>
