<p><ul><li>Normal (130℃, 135℃, 140℃)</li><li>Medium (150℃, 155℃)</li><li>High (above 170℃)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-Tg PCB boards are categorized into three main market types based on their glass transition temperatures (Tg). Normal Tg boards have Tg values ranging from 130℃ to 140℃, while Medium Tg boards have higher Tg values ranging from 150℃ to 155℃. High Tg boards have the highest Tg values, typically above 170℃. These different market types cater to varying levels of thermal stress and mechanical strength requirements in applications such as aerospace, automotive, and industrial electronics.</p></p>
