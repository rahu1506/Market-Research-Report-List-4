<p><p>Pigments for plastics market is divided into two main types: inorganic pigments and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are synthetically produced minerals that offer excellent heat stability and chemical resistance. They are commonly used in applications requiring durability and lightfastness. On the other hand, organic pigments are carbon-based compounds known for their vibrant colors and transparency. They are preferred for their high tinting strength and compatibility with various plastic resins. Both types of pigments play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal and performance of plastic products.</p></p>
