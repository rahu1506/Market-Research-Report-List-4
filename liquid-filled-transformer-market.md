<p><p>Liquid filled transformers are classified into different types based on the type of liquid used for insulation and cooling. The main types include seed oil filled, mineral oil filled, and others. Seed oil filled transformers use natural ester oil as the insulating and cooling fluid, offering environmentally friendly and fire-resistant properties. Mineral oil filled transformers use mineral oil, providing good dielectric properties. Other liquid filled transformers may include silicone oil filled transformers or synthetic ester oil filled transformers, each offering unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
