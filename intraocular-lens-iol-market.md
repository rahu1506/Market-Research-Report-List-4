<p><p>Intraocular Lens (IOL) market can be divided into two main types: Non-foldable lenses and Foldable Intraocular Lens. Non-foldable lenses are rigid and are typically used in cases where the eye structure may not support a foldable lens. Foldable Intraocular Lenses, on the other hand, are flexible and can be easily inserted into the eye through a smaller incision, resulting in quicker recovery and reduced risk of complications. Both types of IOLs are crucial in restoring vision after cataract surgery.</p></p>
