<p><p>Air-water heat pumps are classified into three main types: low temperature, high temperature, and hybrid. Low temperature heat pumps are designed for heating systems with lower water temperature requirements, making them suitable for underfloor heating. High temperature heat pumps are used in systems requiring higher water temperatures, such as hot water production. Hybrid heat pumps combine air-water and water-water heat pump technologies for increased efficiency and flexibility in various heating applications. These different types meet the diverse needs of consumers in the heating market.</p></p>
