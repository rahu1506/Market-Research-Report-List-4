<p><p>Data center colocation services can be divided into two main market types: retail colocation and wholesale colocation. Retail colocation is characterized by smaller space requirements, typically below 10 racks, and includes managed services such as security and maintenance. It is popular among businesses looking for flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, wholesale colocation caters to larger space requirements, often involving entire data halls or floors, and offers more customizable solutions for large enterprises or cloud service providers.</p></p>
