<p><p>Mechanical Circulatory Support (MCS) devices are used to help support the heart in patients with severe heart failure. There are different types of MCS devices available in the market, including Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVAD), Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVAD), Biventricular Assist Devices (BiVAD), and Total Artificial Hearts (TAH). LVADs help support the left side of the heart, RVADs support the right side, BiVADs support both sides, and TAHs completely replace the function of the heart. Each type of device caters to different needs and severity of heart failure in patients.</p></p>
