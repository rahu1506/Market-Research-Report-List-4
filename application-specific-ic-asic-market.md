<p><p>Application Specific IC (ASIC) Market Types include full-custom design, standard-cell based, and gate-array based designs. Full-custom design involves designing every aspect of the IC from scratch for optimal performance but is costly and time-consuming. Standard-cell based designs use pre-designed cells for faster development, while gate-array based designs use pre-defined logic blocks for flexibility and customization. Each type caters to different needs and trade-offs in terms of cost, time-to-market, and performance.</p></p>
