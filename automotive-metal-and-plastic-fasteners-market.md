<p><p>Automotive metal and plastic fasteners market includes various types such as bolts, nuts, rivets, screws, washers, and other fastening components. Bolts are used for holding two or more materials together through a threaded hole. Nuts are used to secure bolts in place. Rivets are permanent fasteners used to join materials together. Screws are threaded fasteners that create a tight hold. Washers are used to distribute the load of a fastener and prevent damage. Other fasteners include clips, pins, and retaining rings.</p></p>
