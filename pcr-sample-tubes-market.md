<p><strong>The PCR Sample Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-bottom Tubes</li><li>Cylindrical Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCR sample tubes are available in two main types: V-bottom tubes and cylindrical tubes. V-bottom tubes have a conical shape at the bottom, which facilitates the sedimentation of particles during centrifugation, making them ideal for applications such as DNA extraction and purification. On the other hand, cylindrical tubes have a flat bottom, providing stability during PCR reactions and storage. Both types of tubes are commonly used in molecular biology laboratories for various PCR applications.</p></p>
