<p><p>Variable Refrigerant Volume (VRV) System market can be segmented into two types: Heat Recovery System and Heat Pump System. Heat Recovery System allows for simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones, maximizing energy efficiency. Heat Pump System uses a single refrigerant circuit to transfer heat between indoor and outdoor units, making it an ideal choice for spaces requiring either heating or cooling. Both systems offer advanced control and flexibility, making them popular choices for commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
