<p><p>Cassette seal equipment comes in two main types: single cassette seals and dual tandem cassette seals. Single cassette seals are designed to prevent leakage in industrial equipment using a single seal structure. On the other hand, dual tandem cassette seals consist of two sets of sealing elements working in tandem to provide extra protection against leaks. Both types of cassette seals are widely used in various industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and pharmaceutical for ensuring the reliable operation of rotating equipment.</p></p>
