<p><p>The Visual Effects (VFX) market consists of various types, including Simulation FX, Animation, Modelling, Matte Painting, and Compositing. Simulation FX involves creating realistic effects such as water, fire, and explosions. Animation involves bringing characters and objects to life through movement. Modelling is the creation of 3D objects and environments. Matte Painting is the technique of creating realistic backgrounds for scenes. Compositing involves combining visual elements to create a cohesive final image. These different types work together to create visually stunning effects in film, television, and other media.</p></p>
