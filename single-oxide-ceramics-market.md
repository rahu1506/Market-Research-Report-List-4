<p><p>Single oxide ceramics market is categorized into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I includes ceramics with a high level of purity and homogeneity, making them suitable for applications requiring excellent thermal and electrical properties. On the other hand, Type II ceramics have lower purity and may contain impurities, but offer superior mechanical strength and wear resistance. Type I ceramics are primarily used in high-tech industries such as electronics and aerospace, while Type II ceramics are commonly found in industrial applications like cutting tools and bearings.</p></p>
