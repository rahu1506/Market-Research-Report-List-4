<p><p>Liquid formulations drug delivery market includes two main types: suspensions and solutions. Suspensions consist of solid particles dispersed in a liquid medium, requiring shaking before use. Solutions are homogeneous mixtures of drug molecules dissolved in a liquid, which do not need shaking prior to administration. These types offer various advantages such as improved bioavailability, ease of administration, and increased patient compliance. The market for liquid formulations continues to grow, driven by the demand for more effective and convenient drug delivery methods.</p></p>
