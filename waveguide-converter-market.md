<p><p>Waveguide converters are devices used for converting different waveguide modes. There are three main types of waveguide converters - Transverse Electric (TE) converters, Transverse Magnetic (TM) converters, and Transverse Electric and Magnetic (TEM) converters. TE converters are designed to convert between different TE modes in the waveguide, while TM converters convert between different TM modes. TEM converters, on the other hand, are capable of converting between both TE and TM modes in the waveguide. Each type of converter serves a specific purpose in signal processing and communication applications.</p></p>
