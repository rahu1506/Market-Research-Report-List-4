<p><p>The air duct cleaning service market can be categorized into two main types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems refer to cleaning services that cover the entire ductwork network in a house, ensuring thorough ventilation and air quality improvement throughout the entire home. Split systems, on the other hand, focus on cleaning the ductwork of individual HVAC units, which are typically found in apartments or smaller living spaces. Both types of services aim to remove dust, debris, and contaminants from the ductwork to improve indoor air quality.</p></p>
