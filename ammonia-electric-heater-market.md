<p><p>Ammonia electric heaters come in two main types: hairpin type and solenoid type. Hairpin type heaters have a hairpin-shaped heating element that efficiently heats the ammonia. Solenoid type heaters use a solenoid coil to generate heat. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications where a reliable and efficient heating source is needed. Hairpin type heaters are known for their durability and long lifespan, while solenoid type heaters are valued for their precise temperature control and fast heating capabilities.</p></p>
