<p><strong>The Single-mode Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) Technology</li><li>Thin-film Filter Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-mode Fiber Coupler market consists of two main types: Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) Technology and Thin-film Filter Technology. FBT technology involves melting and fusing two fibers together to create a coupling effect, offering cost-effective solutions. On the other hand, Thin-film Filter Technology uses thin layers of glass to reflect or transmit certain wavelengths, providing precise coupling functions. Both technologies cater to the high-demand telecommunications industry, offering quality and reliability in single-mode fiber coupling applications.</p></p>
