<p><p>High performance butterfly valves are categorized into two main types - wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are designed to fit between two flanges without the need for additional bolts, while lug type valves have threaded inserts on both sides for easy installation and maintenance. Both types are commonly used in various industries for controlling and regulating flow of liquids and gases. They are highly efficient, durable, and reliable, making them a popular choice for high performance applications.</p></p>
