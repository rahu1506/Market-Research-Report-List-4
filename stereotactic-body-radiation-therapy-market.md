<p><p>Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy (SBRT) is a targeted treatment that delivers high doses of radiation to cancerous tumors with pinpoint accuracy. The two main types of machines used for SBRT are Linear Accelerator (LINAC) Machines and Proton Beam (charged particle) therapy. LINAC machines use X-rays to deliver radiation, while Proton Beam therapy uses proton particles. Both technologies are highly effective in treating various types of cancer, with LINAC machines being more widely available and Proton Beam therapy offering precise treatment with minimal damage to surrounding healthy tissues.</p></p>
