<p><p>IoT Cloud Platform Market consists of three main types of deployment models: Private, Public, and Hybrid. Private deployment model involves the infrastructure being owned and operated by a single organization, ensuring complete control over data and security. Public deployment model offers cloud services to multiple organizations on a pay-as-you-go basis, making it cost-effective and scalable. Hybrid deployment model combines elements of both private and public models, providing flexibility in managing data and applications across different environments.</p></p>
