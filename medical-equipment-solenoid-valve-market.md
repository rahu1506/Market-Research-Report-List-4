<p><strong>The Medical Equipment Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Equipment Solenoid Valve Market includes two main types: Direct Acting and Pilot Operated valves. Direct Acting valves operate directly through the electromagnetic force generated by the solenoid, providing a quick response time and simple design. Pilot Operated valves use the solenoid to control a pilot valve, which then actuates the main valve. This allows for larger flow rates and pressures to be controlled with less energy consumption. Both types of valves play a crucial role in controlling the flow of fluids in medical equipment applications.</p></p>
