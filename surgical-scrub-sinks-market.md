<p><p>Surgical scrub sinks come in different types based on the number of stations they have. A 1-station scrub sink is designed for single users, while a 2-station scrub sink accommodates two users at the same time. A 3-station scrub sink allows three users to scrub in simultaneously. The choice of the number of stations depends on the volume of users and the flow of patients in the facility. Each type of scrub sink is designed to provide a convenient and efficient space for healthcare workers to scrub in before surgeries.</p></p>
