<p><p>A Fluidized Bed Concentrator (FBC) is a device used for air pollution control, specifically for volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and hazardous air pollutants (HAPs) emissions. The technology utilizes a bed of solid particles that are fluidized by an upward flow of gas for the concentration and removal of contaminants from air streams.</p><p>The Fluidized Bed Concentrator (FBC) market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing awareness about environmental pollution and the need for strict regulations for emission control.
