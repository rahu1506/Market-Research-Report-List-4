<p><p>Handheld Explosives Trace Detector Market includes two main types of technologies: Mass Spectrometry Technology and Ion Mobility Spectrometry Technology. Mass Spectrometry Technology is based on separating and analyzing ions according to their mass-to-charge ratio, providing highly accurate and reliable results. Ion Mobility Spectrometry Technology, on the other hand, relies on separating ions based on their mobility in a carrier gas, offering quick and sensitive detection of explosives. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing security measures by detecting trace amounts of explosives in various environments.</p></p>
