<p><p>Sliding miter saws come in two main market types: single bevel and double bevel. Single bevel saws can tilt in one direction, typically to the left, allowing for angled cuts on one side of the material. Double bevel saws, on the other hand, can tilt in both directions, left and right, making it easier to create angled cuts on both sides of the material without having to flip it over. Both types of saws are popular choices among woodworkers and DIY enthusiasts for their versatility and precision in making accurate cuts.</p></p>
