<p><p>Wafer Temporary Debonder market consists of different types based on the debonding method employed. Thermal Debonding involves the use of heat to break the bond between the wafer and temporary adhesive. Mechanical Debonding utilizes physical force to separate the wafer from the adhesive layer. Laser Debonding utilizes laser technology to remove the adhesive layer. Other methods may include chemical debonding or ultrasonic debonding. Each type offers unique advantages and is used based on specific requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
