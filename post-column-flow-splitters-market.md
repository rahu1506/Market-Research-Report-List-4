<p><p>Post-column flow splitters are available in three different types: double fixed, replaceable fixed, and adjustable. Double fixed flow splitters have two fixed split ratios that cannot be changed. Replaceable fixed flow splitters have fixed split ratios that can be replaced with different ratios when needed. Adjustable flow splitters have a flexible split ratio that can be easily adjusted based on specific requirements. Each type of flow splitter offers unique advantages and flexibility in analytical processes.</p></p>
