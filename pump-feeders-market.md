<p><p>Pump feeders are used in various industries to accurately dispense materials. Volumetric pump feeders operate based on a set volume of material being dispensed at each cycle, making them ideal for precise dosing. Gravitational pump feeders, on the other hand, rely on gravity to move materials through the system, making them suitable for free-flowing materials. Both types of pump feeders play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and controlled material handling processes in different industrial applications.</p></p>
