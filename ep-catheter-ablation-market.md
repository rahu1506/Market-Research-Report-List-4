<p><p>EP Catheter Ablation market includes Radiofrequency Ablation Catheters and Cryoablation EP Catheters. Both types of catheters are used in the treatment of cardiac arrhythmias by targeting specific areas of the heart that are causing irregular heartbeats. These catheters offer minimally invasive treatment options for patients with arrhythmias.</p></p>
