<p><p>Marine hoses such as expandable hoses are a type of flexible tubing commonly used in marine applications for easy storage and transportation. They can extend in length when filled with water and retract back to a compact size when empty. Porous or perforated hoses have small holes throughout their structure, allowing water to seep through slowly. These types of hoses are commonly used in irrigation systems or for drainage purposes in marine environments.</p></p>
