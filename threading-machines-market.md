<p><ul><li>Pillar Type</li><li>Bed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threading machines in the market can be categorized into two main types: pillar type and bed type. Pillar type threading machines are characterized by a vertical spindle mounted on a pillar, offering stability and precision during the threading process. On the other hand, bed type threading machines have a horizontal spindle mounted on a bed, which allows for more flexibility and a wider range of applications. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as manufacturing and construction for threading various materials.</p></p>
