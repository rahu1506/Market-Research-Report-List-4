<p><p>Non-contact linear position sensors with analog output signals provide continuous and smooth measurement of position without physical contact with the object being measured. These sensors are suitable for applications where precise and real-time position data is required. On the other hand, sensors with digital output signals provide position data in discrete and quantized values, making them ideal for applications that require on/off or high-resolution position sensing. Both types of sensors offer reliable and accurate position measurement solutions for various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
