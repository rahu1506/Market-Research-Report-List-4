<p><p>Horizontal Pressure Leaf Filters are industrial filtration systems used for the filtration of liquids in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food and beverages, and mining. These filters consist of a horizontal vessel in which a series of vertically mounted filtration leaves are connected to a manifold at the bottom of the vessel. The liquid to be filtered is pumped into the vessel and passes through the filter leaves, which remove solid particles and impurities from the liquid. The clean liquid is then collected at the bottom of the vessel for further processing.</p><p>The Horizontal Pressure Leaf Filters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
