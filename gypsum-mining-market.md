<p><p>Anhydrite ore deposits, fibrous gypsum deposits, and common gypsum deposits are the three main types of gypsum mining markets. Anhydrite ore deposits typically contain high levels of calcium sulfate and are used in industries such as cement production. Fibrous gypsum deposits are valued for their high quality and strength, making them ideal for use in construction materials. Common gypsum deposits are widely used in industries ranging from agriculture to medicine. There are also other niche markets for gypsum, such as in the production of food additives and soil amendments.</p></p>
