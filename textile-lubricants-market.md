<p><p>Textile lubricants are used in various stages of textile production to reduce friction and improve efficiency. Spin finish lubricants are applied to fibers during spinning to enhance smooth processing. Coning oils are used during winding to prevent breakage and improve yarn quality. Knitting oils are applied during knitting to reduce friction and improve fabric quality. Greases are used for maintenance of textile machinery. Each type of lubricant serves a specific purpose in enhancing the performance and quality of textile production processes.</p></p>
