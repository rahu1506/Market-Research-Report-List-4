Single-lumen extension sets have one tube for medication administration, while double-lumen sets have two tubes for separate medications or fluids. Single-lumen sets are typically used for simpler medication administration, while double-lumen sets are used when multiple medications need to be administered simultaneously. Both types of extension sets provide a needle-free way to connect IV lines and are designed to reduce the risk of needle-stick injuries and contamination.</p></p>
