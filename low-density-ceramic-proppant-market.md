<p><strong>The Low Density Ceramic Proppant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large Mesh</li><li>Small Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Density Ceramic Proppant market can be categorized into two types based on mesh size - Large Mesh and Small Mesh. Large Mesh proppants have bigger particles, typically ranging from 20/40 to 40/70 mesh size, used for high-permeability formations to maintain fracture connectivity. Small Mesh proppants have smaller particles, usually in the range of 70/140 to 100/200 mesh size, used for low-permeability formations to maximize fracture complexity. Both types play a crucial role in hydraulic fracturing operations to enhance oil and gas production.</p></p>
