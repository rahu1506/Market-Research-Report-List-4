<p><p>The Infrared Fence market can be categorized into two main types: Passive Infrared Fence and Active Infrared Fence. Passive Infrared Fence systems detect motion and heat signatures in their field of view, triggering an alarm when these patterns are disrupted. Active Infrared Fence systems emit a beam of infrared light across the perimeter, and an alarm is triggered when this beam is interrupted. Both types of Infrared Fence systems are commonly used for perimeter security in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
