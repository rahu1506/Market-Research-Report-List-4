<p><p>Bolted Bonnet Valves are a type of valve commonly used in industrial applications for their high-pressure and high-temperature resistance. There are three main types of Bolted Bonnet Valves: Check, Globe, and Gate. Check valves are designed to allow flow in only one direction, Globe valves control flow using a plug and seat, while Gate valves use a sliding gate to control flow. These valves are secured with bolts to ensure a tight seal under high pressure.</p></p>
