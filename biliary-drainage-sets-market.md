<p><p>Biliary Drainage Sets include Biliary Drainage Catheters and Biliary Drainage Bags. Biliary Drainage Catheters are used to drain bile from the liver or gallbladder into a drainage bag outside the body. These catheters are inserted through the skin into the bile ducts to relieve blockages or obstructions. Biliary Drainage Bags are medical devices used to collect and store bile that is drained from the body through a catheter. Both these products play a crucial role in managing biliary obstructions and promoting proper liver function.</p></p>
