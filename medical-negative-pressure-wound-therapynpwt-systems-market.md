<p><p>Traditional Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Systems are reusable devices that apply negative pressure to the wound through a vacuum pump system. These systems are cost-effective but require cleaning and maintenance. Single-Use Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Systems are disposable devices that are convenient and reduce the risk of cross-contamination. They offer a more hygienic option for wound care but are typically more expensive than traditional systems. Both types of NPWT systems are used for managing complex wounds and promoting healing.</p></p>
