Urea is a widely used fertilizer due to its high nitrogen content. Ammonium nitrate is known for its high nutrient availability and quick release of nitrogen. Calcium ammonium nitrate (CAN) is a popular choice for crops that require both calcium and nitrogen for optimal growth.</p></p>
