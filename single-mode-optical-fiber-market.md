<p><strong>The Single-mode Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-mode optical fiber markets are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their transmission characteristics. Type I fibers have a larger mode field diameter and are more commonly used for long-distance communication due to their lower dispersion and attenuation rates. In contrast, Type II fibers have a smaller mode field diameter and are typically used for shorter distances, offering higher bandwidth capabilities. Both types cater to specific needs in the telecommunications industry, providing efficient and reliable data transmission solutions.</p></p>
