<p><ul><li>Single effect absorption</li><li>Double effect absorption</li><li>Triple effect absorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorption chillers are categorized based on the number of effects they utilize to create cooling. Single effect absorption chillers use one heat source to generate cooling, making them the simplest and least efficient option. Double effect absorption chillers utilize two stages of absorption, increasing efficiency and performance. Triple effect absorption chillers are the most advanced, utilizing three stages of absorption to achieve the highest level of efficiency and cooling capacity. Each type of absorption chiller caters to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
