<p><p>Pneumatically actuated butterfly valves are commonly available in two main types - wafer type and lug type. Wafer type butterfly valves are designed to fit between flanges and are secured by bolts, making them a compact and lightweight option. Lug type butterfly valves have threaded bolt holes on either side for easy installation and removal without affecting the pipeline. Both types offer efficient flow control and are popular choices in various industries for their reliable performance and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
