<p><p>Solder joint inspection market includes Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI) types. AOI utilizes cameras and software to inspect solder joints for defects such as misalignment or insufficient solder. AXI uses X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints for defects like voids or cracks. Both methods help manufacturers ensure the quality of solder joints in electronic components, improving reliability and reducing the risk of potential failures. These technologies are critical in the electronics manufacturing industry to maintain high standards of quality control.</p></p>
