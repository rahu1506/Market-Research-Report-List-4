<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Infrared Device</li><li>Mid-wave Length Device</li><li>Long-wave Length Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging devices are segmented based on the length of the infrared waves they detect. Short-wave length infrared devices operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range, ideal for medical and surveillance applications. Mid-wave length devices detect in the 3 to 5 micrometer range, commonly used in industrial and military settings. Long-wave length devices, detecting in the 8 to 14 micrometer range, are popular for firefighting and search and rescue operations. Each market segment has unique advantages and applications based on wavelength sensitivity.</p></p>
