<p><p>Bronze gate valves are available in two main market types: Flanged Connection and Threaded Connection. Flanged Connection gate valves are designed with flanges that allow for easy and secure installation by bolting the valve to the pipeline. Threaded Connection gate valves, on the other hand, feature threaded ends that can be easily screwed onto the pipeline for quick and efficient installation. Both market types offer durable and reliable performance for various applications in plumbing, HVAC, and industrial systems.</p></p>
