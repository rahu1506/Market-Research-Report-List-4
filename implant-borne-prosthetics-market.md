<p><p>Implant Borne Prosthetics are categorized into two main types - Bone Level Prosthetics and Tissue Level Prosthetics. Bone Level Prosthetics are attached directly to the jawbone, providing stability and support for the prosthetic tooth. Tissue Level Prosthetics, on the other hand, are attached to the soft tissues surrounding the implant, making them suitable for patients with shallow bone structure. Both types of prosthetics offer effective solutions for individuals seeking to replace missing teeth with durable and long-lasting implants.</p></p>
