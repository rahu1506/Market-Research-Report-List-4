<p><p>Electric clothes dryers can be divided into two main market types - vented and non-vented. Vented electric clothes dryers release hot air outside through a vent, which is typically installed through a wall or window. These dryers are more common and tend to be more efficient. Non-vented electric clothes dryers, on the other hand, do not require a vent and instead recycle the hot air within the dryer. While they are easier to install, they are less energy-efficient and may lead to higher humidity levels in the laundry room.</p></p>
