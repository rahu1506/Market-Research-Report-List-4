There are three main types of aspiration filters - hose filters, pocket filters, and cartridge filters. Hose filters are flexible and easy to install, making them suitable for small spaces. Pocket filters offer higher filtration efficiency and are commonly used in HVAC systems. Cartridge filters provide a large filter surface area and are ideal for high air flow applications. Each type of filter caters to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
