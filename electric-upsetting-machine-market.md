<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric upsetting machines can be categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical electric upsetting machines have a vertical ram movement, which is ideal for compact spaces and applications where the workpiece needs to be held in a fixed position. Horizontal electric upsetting machines, on the other hand, have a horizontal ram movement, which is better suited for larger workpieces or applications where the workpiece needs to be rotated or manipulated during the upsetting process. Both types of machines have their own advantages and are used in various industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
