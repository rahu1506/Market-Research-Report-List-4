<p><p>Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) are used in fiber optic network testing to measure the attenuation and detect faults in the optical fiber. There are three main types of OTDR markets: Bench Top OTDRs are stationary units that offer high precision and accuracy for laboratory settings. Rack Mount OTDRs are designed for larger-scale testing operations in data centers or telecommunication hubs. Handheld OTDRs are portable devices commonly used by field technicians for on-site testing and troubleshooting. Each type of OTDR serves a specific market need based on its functionality and features.</p></p>
