<p><p>Hepatitis B RNA Detection Technology market includes two main types: RT PCR Technology and RNA Isothermal Amplification Technology. RT PCR Technology involves reverse transcription followed by polymerase chain reaction to detect and quantify viral RNA, offering high sensitivity and specificity. On the other hand, RNA Isothermal Amplification Technology directly amplifies RNA at a constant temperature, providing faster results and simpler workflow. Both technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring Hepatitis B infections, catering to different laboratory needs and preferences.</p></p>
