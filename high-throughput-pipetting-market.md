<p><p>High Throughput Pipetting refers to the process of rapidly transferring liquids in laboratory settings. Two main types of High Throughput Pipetting systems are Manual Pipetting System and Electric Pipetting System. Manual Pipetting Systems require human intervention for liquid transfer, while Electric Pipetting Systems automate the process using motorized components. Manual systems are cost-effective but time-consuming, while Electric systems offer higher speed and accuracy. Both systems are essential in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and healthcare for efficient liquid handling.</p></p>
