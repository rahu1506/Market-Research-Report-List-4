<p><p>Roots vacuum pumps are categorized into two main types based on their structure and operation- Single-stage Roots Vacuum Pumps and Multi-stage Roots Vacuum Pumps. Single-stage pumps have a simpler design with a single rotor and are suitable for low to medium vacuum applications. On the other hand, multi-stage pumps have two or more rotors which allow for higher pumping speeds and better efficiency, making them ideal for high vacuum applications. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
