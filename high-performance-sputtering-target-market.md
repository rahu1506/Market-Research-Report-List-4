<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plane targets are stationary sputtering targets typically used in large-scale industrial applications. They offer high deposition rates and uniform film thickness, making them suitable for producing large quantities of thin films. Rotating targets, on the other hand, are used in more specialized applications where higher film quality is required. They provide better material utilization and film uniformity due to their rotating motion during the sputtering process. Both plane and rotating targets cater to different market needs based on size, quality, and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
