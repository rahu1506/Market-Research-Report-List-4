<p><p>Portable speakers can be categorized into two main types: active speakers and passive speakers. Active speakers have a built-in amplifier and power source, making them more convenient and easier to use. They are suitable for consumers looking for a plug-and-play solution. On the other hand, passive speakers rely on an external amplifier to power them, offering more customization options for audio enthusiasts. Both types cater to different market segments based on their preferences for convenience and control over audio setup.</p></p>
