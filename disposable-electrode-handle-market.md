<p><p>Disposable electrode handles come in two main types: finger switch and fingerless switch. Finger switch handles require the user to press a button with their finger to activate the electrode, while fingerless switch handles are activated by simply touching the skin. The finger switch type offers more precise control over when the electrode is in use, while the fingerless switch type is more convenient for quick and easy application. Both types cater to different preferences and needs within the disposable electrode handle market.</p></p>
