<p><p>Power MOSFETs for the automotive market come in three main types: N-channel, P-channel, and Dual-channel. </p><p>N-channel MOSFETs are widely used due to their high efficiency and low on-resistance. They are ideal for applications where a high current and voltage are required. P-channel MOSFETs are used in applications where a negative voltage supply is needed. Dual-channel MOSFETs combine both N-channel and P-channel MOSFETs in a single package, providing designers with flexibility and space-saving options for their automotive applications.</p></p>
