<p><p>Micro Remote Operated Vehicles are categorized into five main classes based on their functionalities and capabilities. Class I consists of pure observation vehicles used for surveillance purposes. Class II vehicles combine observation with the option to carry payloads. Class III vehicles are work class vehicles designed for performing tasks underwater. Class IV vehicles are seabed-working vehicles used for construction or maintenance. Class V includes prototype or development vehicles used for testing new technologies and advancements in the field. Each class serves a different purpose in the market of micro ROVs.</p></p>
