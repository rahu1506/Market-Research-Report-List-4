<p><p>Cystoscopes are devices used by medical professionals to examine the bladder and urethra. There are two main types of cystoscopes: rigid and flexible. Rigid cystoscopes are inflexible and typically have a straight tube with a light and camera at one end. Flexible cystoscopes, on the other hand, have a bendable tube that can navigate the curves of the urinary tract more easily. Both types of cystoscopes are important tools in diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the bladder and urethra.</p></p>
