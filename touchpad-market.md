<p><p>Traditional touchpads are the original form of touchpads with physical buttons for clicking and scrolling. They offer a tactile feedback to users, allowing for precise control over cursor movements. On the other hand, buttonless touchpads are sleeker in design and do not have physical buttons. Instead, they rely on gestures and taps for clicking and scrolling. Buttonless touchpads provide a more seamless and intuitive user experience, but some users may prefer the traditional touchpad for its tactile feedback.</p></p>
