<p><p>Hydroformed metal bellows are used in various industries and applications, with two main market types: single wall and multi-wall bellows. Single wall bellows consist of a single layer of metal formed into a bellows shape, providing flexibility and durability for applications requiring basic pressure and movement compensation. In contrast, multi-wall bellows have multiple layers of metal stacked upon each other, offering increased flexibility, strength, and resistance to pressure and movement. Both market types cater to different needs and specifications in various industries.</p></p>
