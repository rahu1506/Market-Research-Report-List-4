<p><p>The Artificial Organ & Bionics Market includes Mechanical Bionics and Electronic Bionics. Mechanical Bionics are devices that mimic or enhance the capabilities of biological systems through mechanical components. Examples include prosthetic limbs and orthopedic implants. Electronic Bionics, on the other hand, utilize electronic systems to replicate biological functions or enhance them through electrical stimulation. Examples include cochlear implants and pacemakers. Both types of bionics play a crucial role in improving the quality of life for individuals with physical limitations.</p></p>
