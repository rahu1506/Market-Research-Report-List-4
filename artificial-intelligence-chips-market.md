<p><p>Artificial Intelligence Chips Market includes four main types of chips: GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) are highly efficient in handling parallel tasks, making them suitable for machine learning and neural network applications. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are customized chips designed for specific AI tasks. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) offer flexibility for reprogramming and adapting to different AI algorithms. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are general-purpose chips that are used for a wide range of computing tasks but are not as efficient as GPUs or ASICs for AI applications.</p></p>
