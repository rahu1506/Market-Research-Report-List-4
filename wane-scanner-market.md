<p><p>Both Side Scan and Single Side Scan are two market types in the Wane Scanner industry. Both Side Scan systems have sensors on both sides of the material being scanned, providing a more comprehensive analysis of the material's dimensions and defects. Single Side Scan systems, on the other hand, only have sensors on one side of the material, making them more cost-effective but providing a less detailed scan. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and budget of the customer.</p></p>
