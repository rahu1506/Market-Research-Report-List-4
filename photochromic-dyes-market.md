<p><p>Photochromic dyes market consists of various types such as Azobenzenes, Spiropyrans, Diarylethenes, Fulgides, Hexaarylbiimidazoles, and Spiroperimidines. Azobenzenes are widely used for their reversible color changes under UV light. Spiropyrans switch between colorless and colored forms with light exposure. Diarylethenes exhibit fast switching properties and high fatigue resistance. Fulgides are known for their long-lived color states. Hexaarylbiimidazoles offer high thermal stability and color changes with UV light. Spiroperimidines are used for their photochromic behavior and unique structural properties.</p></p>
