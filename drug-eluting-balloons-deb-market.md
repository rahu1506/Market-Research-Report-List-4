<p><p>Drug Eluting Balloons (DEBs) are a type of medical device used in minimally invasive procedures to treat coronary and peripheral artery disease. Peripheral DEBs are used to treat blockages in arteries outside the heart, such as in the legs, while Coronary DEBs are used in the treatment of blockages in the arteries of the heart. Both types of DEBs deliver medication directly to the site of the blockage, helping to prevent restenosis and improve outcomes for patients undergoing these procedures.</p></p>
