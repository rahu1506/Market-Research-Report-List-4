<p><p>Capacitive inclinometers are sensors used to measure the tilt of an object in either a single axis or dual axis configuration. Single axis capacitive inclinometers measure tilt along one direction, while dual axis capacitive inclinometers measure tilt along two perpendicular directions simultaneously. Single axis inclinometers are typically used for simpler applications such as monitoring the tilt of a surface, while dual axis inclinometers are used for more complex applications that require measurement of tilt in multiple directions.</p></p>
