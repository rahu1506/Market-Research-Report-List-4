<p><p>Packaging metallized film market can be categorized into three main types - PET film, CPP film, and BOPP film. PET film offers high tensile strength and excellent barrier properties, making it ideal for food packaging. CPP film provides good heat sealing and moisture barrier properties, suitable for snack and confectionery packaging. BOPP film is known for its clarity and stiffness, making it suitable for labels and graphics. Each type of metallized film serves different packaging needs based on their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
