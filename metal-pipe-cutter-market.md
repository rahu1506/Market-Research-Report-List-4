<p><p>Metal pipe cutters can be categorized into two main types based on their operation: manual and automatic. Manual pipe cutters require physical effort from the user to operate, rotating a handle to cut through the metal pipe. On the other hand, automatic pipe cutters are powered by a motor, significantly reducing the physical effort required from the user. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with manual cutters being more cost-effective and portable, while automatic cutters offer convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
