Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology offers accurate indoor positioning and asset tracking. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) technology provides highly precise location data for applications like asset management and navigation. Wi-Fi positioning systems use existing Wi-Fi networks to track assets or individuals within a building. Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) technology employs radio waves to track and identify objects, making it ideal for inventory management and supply chain optimization. Each technology has unique strengths and applications within the micro-location market.</p></p>
