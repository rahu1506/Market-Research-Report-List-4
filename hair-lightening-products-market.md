<p><p>Hair lightening products can be categorized into two main types: artificial products and natural products. Artificial products typically contain harsh chemicals such as hydrogen peroxide or ammonia that strip pigment from the hair strands to achieve a lighter shade. On the other hand, natural products use gentler ingredients like lemon juice, honey, or chamomile to gradually lighten the hair without causing as much damage. Consumers can choose between these two market types based on their preferences for effectiveness, speed, and potential risks to hair health.</p></p>
