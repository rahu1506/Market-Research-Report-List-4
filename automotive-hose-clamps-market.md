<p><p>The automotive hose clamps market can be broadly classified into four types: Screw/Band (Worm Gear) Clamps, Spring Clamps, Wire Clamps, and Others. Screw/Band Clamps are the most common type, featuring a screw mechanism to tighten the clamp around the hose. Spring Clamps rely on the spring tension to secure the hose. Wire Clamps use a wire to tighten the hose, providing a secure grip. The "Others" category may include specialty clamps with unique designs or materials for specific applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
