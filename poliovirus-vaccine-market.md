<p><p>The Poliovirus vaccine market includes two main types - Inactivated Polio Vaccine (IPV) and Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV). IPV is given as an injection and contains killed poliovirus, providing immunity without causing the disease. OPV is administered orally and contains weakened live poliovirus, stimulating the immune system to create antibodies. Although both vaccines are effective in preventing polio, their usage and availability may vary based on factors such as cost, accessibility, and specific vaccination requirements in different regions.</p></p>
